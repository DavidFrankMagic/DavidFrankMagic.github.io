--- layout: null section-type: about title: About --- ## About David is a renowned magician and mentalist with over a decade of experience captivating global audiences with his unique blend of world-class sleight of hand and corporate expertise. Based in the heart of New York City, he's a sought-after act for event planners across the country. In addition to his magical talents, David is currently employed at Goldman Sachs and brings a wealth of knowledge and experience from the corporate world. He holds a B.S. in Applied Economics and Management and Finance from Cornell University and is a published author of multiple peer-reviewed economics research papers that have even been cited by the Federal Reserve. At Cornell, David founded and led the Cornell Magic Society and created two university-accredited courses on the "Art of Magic: From Technique to Performance" and the "History of Magic." His unique blend of business savvy and magical prowess has earned him a spotlight in The Economist and the Cornell Daily Sun. Through David's original and witty performances, he brings a touch of magic to corporate events, galas, and private parties. His ability to seamlessly integrate magic into any event has earned him a reputation as one of the most versatile and sought-after magicians in the industry. Whether he's performing for a room full of executives or a casual gathering of friends, David's engaging and interactive performances are sure to leave a lasting impression on guests. With his professionalism, attention to detail, and captivating personality, he'll make your event an unforgettable experience.
